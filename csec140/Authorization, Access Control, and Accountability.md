

There are 4 main access control polices 
- DAC(Discretionary Access Control)
	- Based on the identity of the subject 
- MAC(Mandotory Access Control)
	- used in government agencies or military
	- based on sensitivity of the information contained in the object as represented by a security level
- RBAC(Role-based Access Control)
	- permitted actions on resources are identified with roles rather than with individual subject identites
- ABAC(Attribute-based access control)
	- based of attributes of the subject
